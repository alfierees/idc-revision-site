## ⚠️ Answer conflicts across papers

> [!warning] Four questions were marked differently on different papers
> Where a majority agrees, trust the majority; verify against this year's slides.

1. **[[Rayport Spheres]]** — P1 & P3 mark **Public / Social / Tribal / Psychological** (correct); P6 marks "Professional / Family / Tribal / Psychological" (**likely an error**). → Go with **Public / Social / Tribal / Psychological**.
2. **[[GDN]] publisher incentive** — P2 & P3 mark **"higher certainty of ad revenue"**; P5 marks "increases adaptation to user interests." → Go with **revenue certainty** (the publisher's own incentive).
3. **Bonchek "future challenge"** — P3 & P5 mark **"lack of data availability"**; P4 marks "difficulty adapting to technologies." → Go with **lack of data availability** (2 of 3).
4. **High Value Features** — P5 marks "**Value** ↔ **Want**"; P6 marks "**Value** ↔ **Need**." → Genuinely split; **check the exact slide**.
