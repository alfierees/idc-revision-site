An **unconditional right to consideration** — an amount billed and owed by the customer, where only the passage of time is required before payment. Distinct from a contract asset ([[Income Receivable]]), which is a *conditional* right (still dependent on something other than time), and from [[Deferred Revenue]], a liability for cash received before delivery.

See [[Session 8 - Revenue Recognition|Session 8]].
