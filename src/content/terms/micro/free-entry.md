The absence of entry/exit barriers; firms enter while profit is positive and exit while it is negative, which is what drives the [[Zero-Profit Condition]] in the [[Long-Run Equilibrium]].

See [[Topic 2 - Equilibrium in Different Market Structures|Topic 2]].
