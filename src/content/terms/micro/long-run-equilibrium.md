With [[Free Entry]] and exit, economic profit is competed to **zero**: price settles at minimum average cost and each firm produces at its [[Minimum Efficient Scale|efficient scale]]. Demand then fixes only the *number* of firms, not the price (constant-cost industry).

See [[Topic 2 - Equilibrium in Different Market Structures|Topic 2]].
