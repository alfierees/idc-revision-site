An insurance premium equal to the insurer's expected pay-out, so the insurer breaks even; under [[Moral Hazard]] it is computed on the *insured* (over-consumed) quantities, so it exceeds the consumer's expected uninsured spending.

See [[Topic 1 - Asymmetric Information|Topic 1]].
