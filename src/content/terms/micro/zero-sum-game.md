A game of pure conflict where one player's gain is exactly the other's loss (e.g. matching-pennies / odd-or-even); typically has no pure-strategy Nash Equilibrium but a unique [[Mixed Strategy]] one.

See [[Topic 3 - Game Theory|Topic 3]].
