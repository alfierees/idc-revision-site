A firm operates in the short run only if price covers average *variable* cost, $P \ge \min AVC$; otherwise it shuts down. Fixed costs are sunk, so losses are tolerated as long as variable costs are covered.

See [[Topic 2 - Equilibrium in Different Market Structures|Topic 2]].
