Streaming Models — How streaming services monetise: **SVOD** (subscription, e.g. Netflix), **AVOD** (ad-supported), **TVOD** (transactional/pay-per-title), **HVOD** (hybrid) and **FAST** (Free Ad-Supported Streaming TV, linear-style channels).
