> [!example] Open-ended question (not in the 2026 MCQ-only format)
> **Q18 (15 pts).** In July 2023, Meta launched **Threads**, leveraging Instagram for the onboarding process (an Instagram account is required, and users can "import" their followers and contacts). As a digital-marketing expert, analyse this go-to-market strategy with at least two advantages and two disadvantages, plus a conclusion and recommendation.
>
> **Advantages:** instant network effect and low CAC by reusing Instagram's existing social graph for onboarding.
> **Disadvantages:** heavy dependence on Instagram, and weak standalone value/retention if Threads offers little beyond the imported graph.
> **Recommendation:** build differentiated standalone value and retention hooks so Threads is not purely reliant on Instagram.
