## To find a Nash equilibrium

1. Write the payoff matrix.
2. For each cell, mark best responses.
3. A cell where both responses are marked is a Nash equilibrium.

### Steps for iterated dominance

1. Identify a strictly dominated strategy for one player.
2. Eliminate it from the matrix.
3. Repeat until no strictly dominated strategies remain.
4. The surviving strategies form the iterated-dominance solution.
